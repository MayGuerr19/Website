Meet the Owner

Andrea Guerrero Chino

My name is Andrea Guerrero Chino, and I am the proud owner of my first barbershop. At 27 years old, I began my career in barbering at the age of 19, and I have gained seven years of experience in this craft. As a mother of one, I am incredibly excited to embark on this journey of entrepreneurship.

My specialties include flat tops, beard work, razor cuts, and fades and etc.... I look forward to serving my community and providing quality haircuts!
